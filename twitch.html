Meow Bot - Twitch Counter Features

This bot automatically tracks every "meow" in your stream and lets viewers compete for the top meower spot.

What the Bot Does:

Counts every "meow" typed in chat (works with meow, MEOW, meoooow, etc.)
Tracks personal and channel statistics
Creates leaderboards for your channel and across all channels
Remembers all meow data permanently
Works 24/7, even when you're offline

Chat Commands:

!meow - Shows your total meow count (all time) in the current channel
!top - Shows the top 5 meowers of all time in the current channel
!global - Shows the global leaderboard of streamers across all channels
!requestbot - Provides instructions for adding the bot to a channel
!optinglobal - Opts the channel into the global leaderboard (broadcaster only)
!botinfo - Shows bot creator information and contact details
!help - Shows all available commands
