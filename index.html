😺 Meow Bot Features:

  • 🐾 Meow & Bark Counting: Keep track of your playful nonsense.
  • 🌐 Global Leaderboards: Track your meows across all servers, not just one!
  • 💌 Welcome Messages: Customizable greetings for new members—toggle on or off.
  • ⏰ Personal Reminders: Never forget important stuff - set reminders for yourself.
  • 🧾 Quote Maker: Save and share memorable quotes (beta, work in progress).
    — If you have Make It A Quote you can use /randomquote to pull up random quotes from your quote channel meaning they won't get buried as time goes by.
  • 🎭 Anonymous Confessions: Submit confessions anonymously; admins can enable or disable this feature.
  • 📊 Polls: Create interactive polls with up to 10 options.
  • 🎮 Twitch Alerts: Get notifications for your favorite streamers (feature can be toggled).
  • 🎭 Reaction roles: Also in beta/work in progress.
  • 🧹 /clear Command: Purge up to 100 messages at once for easy chat cleanup.
  • 🖼️ /avatar: Show anyone's Discord avatar in full size.
